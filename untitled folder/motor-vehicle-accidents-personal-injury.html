Motor Vehicle Accidents and Personal Injury

Injury victims have a personal understanding of the lasting effects of dangerous negligence. A serious car accident can leave victims with hospital bills, ongoing physical therapy and major work-life disruptions. And too often a negligent party does not want to take responsibility; they may be unwilling to cover the extensive costs and suffering that was caused by their negligence.

At Sung & Hwang LLP, we will work to hold the negligent party accountable for their actions. Our attorneys use their negotiation skills when dealing with insurance companies as counsel for defendants, and their litigation experience to advocate for clients in the courtroom. Our goal is to recover lost damages for those we represent as expeditiously as possible, with the maximum amount of recovery.

What We Do For Our Injured Clients

We help clients recover damages for many types of injuries including:

  • Motor vehicle accidents
  • Pedestrian accidents
  • Hit-and-run accidents and uninsured and underinsured cases
  • Products liability
  • Premises liability, including slip-and fall cases
  • Wrongful death

We negotiate aggressively with opposing parties and insurance carriers to ensure full compensation for injuries and damages. When litigation is necessary, our experienced litigators have a stellar track record of winning at trial.

Injury Victims Deserve Full Compensation

Those we represent often suffer more than just monetary losses. A serious injury or a death in the family can leave victims with years of emotional suffering. Negligent parties can be held financially responsible for the victim's pain and suffering as well. We work to ensure that our clients are completely compensated for their injuries.

Our lawyers help you recover the lost wages and medical bills that accumulate over the span of an injury. We work alongside health care providers to recover lost damages through careful research and record-keeping during the recovery process.

Experienced Personal Injury Representation

It is important to seek experienced representation in a serious personal injury case. We provide solid counsel to our clients after years of experience recovering for personal injury losses. Clients can contact our attorneys for an initial consultation at 410-772-2324, or contact us online. We will charge no fee concerning your personal injury case without a recovery for you.
